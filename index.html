Ravine

Solo Expedition

Your plane has crashed in the Colorado Rockies and now you must survive until rescue arrives.

How to play:

Each turn consists of a foraging phase (🍃) and a night phase (🌕). You will forfeit hearts at the beginning of each turn, equal to the amount of resources that you wish to collect.
Resources can be used to regain hearts (❤️), start a fire (🔥), and other useful actions.
During the Night Phase, you will have an event that will affect you in various ways.
The game ends if you have no more lives, or you are rescued.
Good Luck!
